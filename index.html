About Us.

We are a team of creative, diverse and highly skilled curriculum designers, workshop facilitators, trainers, program and organizational development professionals. We are committed to accessible healthy sexuality and sexual violence prevention programs for youth and adults

Why choose Us.

With decades of experience in the nonprofit sector, we have a first-hand understanding of the challenges and constraints organizations face. Think of us as part of your team, but without having to invest in multiple full-time staff. You benefit from decades of experience and our range of expertise to execute programs, train facilitators, collect data, write curriculum, and so much more.

Our Mission and Vision.

Mission:
Tailored consulting services designed to help healthy sexuality initiatives sprout, grow and thrive.

Vision:
 A world where all people, of all identities, are affirmed and empowered to live self-determined sexual lives.

What we Do.

We believe in sexual health education that is affirming for people of all body sizes, sexual orientations, genders, races, religions, occupations, socioeconomic circumstances, ages, and abilities. We bring that spirit to our breadth of services, including facilitator training, curriculum design, strengths-based leadership, and program development.

Your needs are constantly changing; navigating strategic change is our specialty!

Services.

Our mix-and-match suite of services is designed to fill critical gaps and build your internal capacity. Sprive meets all your needs in program development and evaluation, comprehensive practitioner training, curriculum design, strategic planning and strengths-based leadership.

Curriculum Development

Our curriculum design approach is rooted in research on effective sexual violence prevention and healthy sexuality education. We work with you to customize responsive, inclusive, and developmentally appropriate content.

Curriculum Implementation

Our seasoned consultants can fill in when you are short on curriculum facilitators or need help with implementation planning.

Curriculum/Facilitator Training

Whether you need beginner or advanced facilitation skills training, we help your team build skills for sexual violence prevention and healthy sexuality workshops with youth and adults.

Program Evaluation

Program Development and Implementation

From your foundation to your strategic plan, we create systems to streamline outreach, logistics, communications, and infrastructure for growth.
